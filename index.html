About

Background

I'm a finance graduate joining Isio's DC Investments division in London in September 2025, with a deep interest in corporate credit markets, real asset financing, and the UK energy transition.

Outside of work, I build independent research and financial models on sectors I find genuinely compelling — currently focused on data centre infrastructure and offshore wind project finance. Both sit at the intersection of long-duration capital deployment and structural market shifts I want to understand deeply.

I'm particularly interested in how corporate refinancing dynamics and debt market conditions shape investment decisions across infrastructure assets.
